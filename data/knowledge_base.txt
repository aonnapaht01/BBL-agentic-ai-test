INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL POLICY

All international business travel must receive prior written approval from the employee's direct manager and the Department Head at least 14 business days before the intended departure date. Employees traveling internationally on behalf of the company are entitled to a daily per diem allowance of USD 120 for meals and incidental expenses when traveling to standard-cost destinations, and USD 180 for high-cost destinations including but not limited to Tokyo, London, Zurich, and New York City. Economy-class airfare is the default booking class for all flights under 6 hours; business-class may be approved for flights exceeding 6 hours with prior Department Head authorization. Hotel accommodations must be booked through the company's approved travel management platform and shall not exceed USD 250 per night for standard destinations or USD 400 per night for high-cost destinations. All travel-related expenses must be submitted with original receipts via the expense management system within 15 business days of return. Failure to submit expenses within the deadline may result in forfeiture of reimbursement. The company does not reimburse expenses related to personal travel extensions, minibar charges, or entertainment not directly related to business objectives.

REMOTE AND HYBRID WORK POLICY

Eligible employees may request a hybrid or fully remote work arrangement subject to approval by their direct manager and the Human Resources department. Under the hybrid model, employees are expected to be physically present in the office a minimum of two days per week, specifically on designated team collaboration days as determined by each department. Core working hours for all remote and hybrid employees are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone, during which employees must be available for meetings, calls, and real-time collaboration. Employees approved for remote work are entitled to a one-time hardware allowance of up to THB 15,000 for the purchase of ergonomic office equipment including a monitor, keyboard, mouse, and desk chair. Recurring monthly internet subsidy of THB 500 is provided upon submission of the internet service invoice. Remote work arrangements are reviewed quarterly and may be modified or revoked based on performance metrics, team needs, or business requirements. Employees must maintain a secure and private workspace and comply with all company data security and confidentiality policies while working remotely. Any change in the remote work location, particularly to a different province or country, must be reported to HR and approved in advance due to potential tax and legal implications.

ANNUAL LEAVE AND WELFARE BENEFITS POLICY

Full-time employees are entitled to a minimum of 10 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, increasing by one additional day for each completed year of service up to a maximum of 20 days. Unused annual leave may be carried over to the following year up to a maximum of 5 days; any leave balance exceeding this carry-over limit will be forfeited on March 31 of the subsequent year unless otherwise approved by the Division Head in writing. Employees must submit annual leave requests at least 5 business days in advance for periods of 3 or more consecutive days, and at least 2 business days in advance for shorter absences. The company provides comprehensive group medical insurance covering outpatient, inpatient, and dental treatment with an annual limit of THB 100,000 per employee for outpatient services and THB 500,000 for inpatient hospitalization. Dependents including spouse and children under 20 years of age may be added to the insurance plan with a 50% company co-payment on the additional premium. Employees are also entitled to an annual health check-up fully subsidized by the company at designated partner hospitals. In addition, the company offers a provident fund with employer matching contributions of up to 5% of the employee's monthly base salary, subject to the employee's voluntary enrollment and a minimum contribution of 3% from the employee's side.

DATA SECURITY AND ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

All employees must adhere to the company's data security standards, which include using company-approved devices and software for all work-related activities. Personal devices may only access corporate systems if they have been registered with the IT department and meet the minimum security requirements, including full-disk encryption, up-to-date antivirus software, and biometric or multi-factor authentication enabled. Employees are prohibited from storing classified or confidential company data on personal cloud storage services such as personal Google Drive, Dropbox, or iCloud accounts. All sensitive documents must be stored on the company's designated secure cloud platform or on-premises encrypted servers. Violations of the data security policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment and potential legal proceedings if a data breach occurs due to negligence. Employees must report any suspected security incident or data breach to the IT Security team within 2 hours of discovery via the designated incident reporting channel.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING POLICY

The company allocates an annual professional development budget of THB 30,000 per employee for external training courses, certifications, and conference attendance relevant to the employee's role and career growth plan. Requests for professional development funding must be submitted through the Learning Management System with a written justification and approved by the employee's manager at least 30 days before the event or course start date. Employees who receive company-sponsored certifications or training exceeding THB 50,000 in value are required to sign a training bond committing to remain with the company for a minimum of 12 months following the completion of the training; early departure within this period obligates the employee to reimburse the full training cost on a pro-rata basis. The company also provides access to an online learning platform with over 5,000 courses covering technical skills, leadership, project management, and language proficiency at no additional cost to employees.
